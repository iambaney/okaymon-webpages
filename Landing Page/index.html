Welcome...

...to the Okayverse!

The Metaverse has grown a mind of its own, an Artificial Intelligence. Even more: it's grown an unconscious. It dreams, and the dreams of The Metaverse become real.


Strange creatures appear, familiar like memories, their names and natures on the tip of everyone's tongue. Professor Okay is convinced these creatures, which he calls Okaymon, hold the answers to the mysteries of the Okayverse.

With the duplicitous Dr. Yako (about whom we'll soon learn more), he invented the Okayball to catch wild Okaymon. He needs your help to collect and befriend them, to learn their secrets and push the frontiers of OkayScience. There are even rewards for rare or interesting Okaymon! Mint Okayballs, collect Okaymon, discover the Okayverse!

You've gotta catch at least one of them!

NFTs

10,000 Collectible Creatures

Okaymon is a collection of up to 10,000 AI-generated creatures living as NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. Why "up to 10,000?" The final size of the collection is determined by the choices you make in our unique gamified drop. The rarity of traits is also shaped by your choices. Okaymon are awarded points and over 100 Okaymon will earn rewards!

Learn more about our gamified drop by reading The Official Okaymon Player's Guide

In brief, the collection is released over 5 themed Generations of 2,000 Okaymon each. The first Generation is Oasis.

You mint Okayballs for .05 ETH each. They are minting passes which can be exchanged for an Okaymon ("catching" an Okaymon). Okayballs and Okaymon behave differently in different generations. This affects the size of the collection and the rarity of Okaymon. The rarest Okaymon are tracked on our Leaderboard (coming soon!)

Note: Only Gen 1 Okayballs are guaranteed to catch you an Okaymon. There's a small chance with later Gens, which grows significant by Gen 5, that you may mint an Okayball and not be able to catch an Okaymon with it. This is part of the game. Read our Player's Guide to understand why.

The unpredictable results of the gamified drop will shape the fate of The Okayverse!

None of our images are drawn or designed by people; they're 100% machine dream!
