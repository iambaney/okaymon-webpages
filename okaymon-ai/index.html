The Metaverse has grown a mind of its own, an Artificial Intelligence. Even more: It's grown an unconscious. It dreams, and the dreams of The Metaverse become real.


Strange creatures appear, familiar like memories, their names and natures on the tip of everyone's tongue. Professor Okay is convinced these creatures, which he calls Okaymon, hold the answers to the mysteries of the Okayverse.

With the duplicitous Dr. Yako (about whom we'll soon learn more), he invented the Okayball to catch wild Okaymon. NFTs

10,000 Collectible Creatures

Okayball Contract Okaymon Contract

Okaymon is a collection of 10,000 AI-generated creatures living on the Ethereum blockchain. The collection is released over 5 themed Generations of 2,000 Okaymon each. We are currently in Gen 2: Depths.

You mint Okayballs for .05 ETH each. They are minting passes which can be exchanged for an Okaymon ("catching" an Okaymon). Okayballs and Okaymon behave differently in different Generations, which is the basis for our gamifed drop mechanic. How you decide to mint affects trait rarity, gives Okaymon points, and ranks them on a Leaderboard. In the end, over 200 Okaymon will win prizes, and any Okaymon could win!

For full details, please read our Player's Guide

Note: Only Gen 1 Okayballs are guaranteed to catch you an Okaymon. There's a small chance with later Gens, which grows significantly by Gen 5, that you may mint an Okayball and not be able to catch an Okaymon with it. This is part of the game! Read our Player's Guide to understand why.

The unpredictable results of the gamified drop will shape the fate of The Okayverse!
